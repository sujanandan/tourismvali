Places To Visit In Kasargode

Bekkal Fort

Bekal Fort was built by Shivappa Nayaka of Keladi in 1650 AD, at Bekal. It is the largest fort in Kerala, spreading over 40 acres. The fort appears to emerge from the sea An important feature is the water-tank, magazine and the flight of steps leading to an observation tower built by Tipu Sultan.

Ananthapura Lake Temple

This is the only lake temple in Kerala.An interesting spot to keep in mind while visiting the temple is a cave to the right corner of the lake. According to the local legend, the deity Anantha Padmanabha chose to go all the way to Thiruvananthapuram through that cave

Ranipuram Hills

Ranipuram (also known by its former name Madathumala) is a village and a major tourist attraction in the Kasaragod district of the Indian state of Kerala. Situated at 780 metres (2,560 ft) above mean sea level.
